About Us

Nhue is a 24/7 creative hub built for makers, brands, and dreamers. From photo and video shoots to brand expos, exhibits, rehearsals, conferences, product launches, and content creation — we’re the space where ideas take shape and stories come to life. Designed for flexibility and driven by creativity, Nhue offers an inspiring environment that works around your schedule, not against it. Whatever you’re planning, we’re here — all day, all night.

2021 – Humble Beginnings

Nhue Creative Advertising Corp. was founded in 2021 at the height of the pandemic. It began as a small studio in Mandaluyong, established by a group of friends driven by creativity and vision. Initially known as Hue Creatives MNL, the studio quickly attracted clients ranging from vloggers and models to professionals seeking high-quality portraits and video shoots.

2023 – Expansion and Rebranding

As demand grew, the company expanded to a larger studio in Makati and rebranded as Nhue Creative Advertising Corp. The name represents a “new hue”—symbolizing a fresh wave of creativity that brings vibrance to the arts.

A Distinctive Studio Space

The studio is fully equipped and thoughtfully designed, combining modern functionality with an aesthetic character. Its interiors draw inspiration from the industrial vibe of New York and London, complemented by a Filipino touch in its furniture and details. This unique blend creates a professional yet welcoming environment that inspires both creativity and collaboration.

Evolution of Services

Over time, the company transformed into a full-service creative hub. Its portfolio expanded beyond photography and videography to include:

  • Advertising services
  • Talent management
  • Studio rental and productions

Nhue has collaborated with brands, products, celebrities, singers, international theater actors, and digital creators.

Today

Nhue Creative Advertising Corp. stands as a multi-faceted agency offering:

  • Studio productions
  • Digital advertising
  • Social media management
  • Comprehensive creative solutions
